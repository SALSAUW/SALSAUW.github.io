SALSA UW


South Asian Law Student Association (SALSA) provides a social, academic, and professional network for law students.



ABOUT US

We strive to create a sense of community among students of South Asian heritage as well as those interested in South Asia. We will engage with legal practitioners in the area and build a robust South Asian legal network. We commit to promoting awareness of issues facing our community through events and discussion.

We recognize and appreciate that South Asia is a diverse and distinct region and hope to celebrate its presence in the Pacific Northwest.
